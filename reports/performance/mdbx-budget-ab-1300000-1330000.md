# Coordinated MDBX Commit-Budget A/B: 1,300,000-1,330,000

Captured 2026-07-16 from the verified height-1,300,000 full-state MainNet
checkpoint. Both variants used durable `NoWriteMap` MDBX and sixteen
write-intent readers. The only change was the coordinated projected-change
budget.

## Correctness

- Replay range: heights `1,300,001..1,330,000` (30,000 blocks).
- Both Ledger and StateService reached `1,330,000`.
- Both variants had zero MPT apply failures.
- Both persisted root `0x10875b33bbdcdd1e90271ba871e213f48e34f3c390579110eb0daf3751f58aa1`.
- `seed1.neo.org:10332 getstateroot(1330000)` returned the same root.
- Release `neo-db-probe` reopened both databases and returned the same height
  and root after shutdown.

## Performance

| Budget | MDBX commits | Import time | Overall BPS | Transaction-bearing BPS | Finalization | Commit stage |
|---:|---:|---:|---:|---:|---:|---:|
| 8,192 control | 11 | 243.17 s | 123.37 | 910.87 | 226.43 s | 226.43 s |
| 16,384 candidate | 6 | 226.32 s | 132.55 | 969.34 | 210.64 s | 210.64 s |

The candidate is approximately `6.9%` faster overall and `6.4%` faster on
transaction-bearing blocks for this transaction-dense high-height window.
The durable MDBX commit stage remains the dominant cost; VM execution was
about `11.2 s` in the control and `10.6 s` in the candidate.
